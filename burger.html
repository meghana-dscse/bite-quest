Ingredients:

  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 burger patty (vegetarian or meat-based)
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 1 slice of tomato
  • 1 slice of onion
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise or ketchup
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • Toast the burger bun on a pan.
  • Cook the burger patty until golden brown.
  • Place the patty on the bottom bun and add cheese.
  • Layer with lettuce, tomato, and onion slices.
  • Spread mayonnaise or ketchup on the top bun.
  • Assemble the burger and enjoy your delicious homemade treat!
