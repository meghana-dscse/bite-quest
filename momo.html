Fun Facts About Momos

Momos originated in Tibet and Nepal but have become a popular street food across Asia. They come in various styles, including steamed, fried, and even tandoori. In some regions, momos are considered a comfort food, and their spicy chutney makes them even more addictive!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/2 tsp garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water for kneading

Instructions:

  • Mix the flour with water and knead into a smooth dough. Let it rest for 20 minutes.
  • In a bowl, mix all the vegetables, garlic paste, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  • Roll out small circles of the dough and fill them with the vegetable mixture.
  • Fold and seal the edges to form dumplings.
  • Steam the momos for about 10-12 minutes until they are cooked.
  • Serve hot with spicy chutney or soy sauce!
