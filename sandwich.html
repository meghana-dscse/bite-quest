Fun Facts About Sandwiches

The sandwich is named after John Montagu, the 4th Earl of Sandwich, who popularized eating meat between bread. Today, sandwiches are a staple worldwide, from classic club sandwiches to unique regional variations!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tbsp butter or mayonnaise
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 slice of cooked chicken or paneer
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice.
  • Layer lettuce, tomato slices, cheese, and cooked chicken or paneer.
  • Sprinkle salt and pepper for taste.
  • Top with the other slice of bread.
  • Toast in a pan or sandwich maker until golden brown.
  • Enjoy your delicious homemade sandwich!
