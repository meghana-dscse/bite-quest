Fun Facts About Pasta

Pasta has been a staple food for centuries and is believed to have originated in Italy. However, some historians suggest that Marco Polo brought it from China. There are over 600 pasta shapes worldwide, each designed to hold different types of sauces perfectly!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pasta
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • ½ cup pasta sauce
  • ½ teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon oregano
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  • Boil 2 cups of water in a pot and add pasta.
  • Cook for 7-10 minutes until soft, then drain the water.
  • In the same pot, add butter/oil and pour in the pasta sauce.
  • Mix in the pasta and season with chili flakes and oregano.
  • Top with grated cheese if available.
  • Serve hot and enjoy a quick hostel-style pasta!
