Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water

  • 1 egg (optional, for protein)
  • Chopped veggies (spring onion, capsicum - optional)

Instructions:

  • Boil 1 cup of water in a kettle or on a stove.
  • Add the instant noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Stir in the seasoning packet, soy sauce, and butter.
  • If using an egg, crack it into the pot and stir for a creamy texture.
  • Add chopped veggies and cook for another minute.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your homemade hostel ramen!
